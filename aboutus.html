About

Story

Cakelicious began as a small family-run bakery in the heart of the city, born out of a passion for baking and a love for bringing smiles to people's faces through delicious treats.

Over the years, we've grown from a humble storefront to a beloved local treasure, known for our creative and mouth-watering cakes, pastries, cupcakes, and donuts.

Team

Behind every delightful treat at Cakelicious is a team of dedicated bakers, decorators, and customer service professionals who pour their heart and soul into their work.

From our experienced pastry chefs who craft our signature flavors to our friendly staff who ensure every visit is a memorable one.

Mission

At Cakelicious, our mission is simple: to make every occasion sweeter. We strive to create delectable baked goods that not only taste amazing but also bring joy and celebration to our customers' lives.

Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or just a regular day that needs a little sweetness, we are dedicated to deliver exceptional quality and unforgettable flavors.

Socials

We're more than just quality products - we're a community of over 300 thousand supporters across our socials. Follow us on Instagram for a glimpse into our lifestyle.
